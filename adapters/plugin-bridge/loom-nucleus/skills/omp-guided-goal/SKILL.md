# OMP Guided Goal

Use when the request is broad or underspecified and a short structured interview will produce a concrete goal before work starts.

## Process

1. Ask only the questions needed to bound scope, success criteria, and constraints.
2. Restate the goal, non-goals, and acceptance criteria for confirmation.
3. Stop interviewing once the goal is concrete enough to plan.

## Rules

- Do not enable persistent goal runtime state; this skill is the interview workflow only.
- Do not claim to control OMP runtime state.
- Keep questions minimal and high-signal.
